Meet the Founders

ChowNow is a start up we founded to bring tourists and vistors the best guide to all the Asian food in Chicago's Chinatown. Our website will take you to the best restaurants and places Chicago has to offer. Our website is aims to bring together foodies through a website medium. Those who are on our site will have access to the top ranked restaurants in Chicago’s Chinatown and get information about transportation, types of food, Chinatown Square and Chinatown Gate. On the separate tabs, we have information about us and reviews about the types of food.   We hope you all enjoy!
